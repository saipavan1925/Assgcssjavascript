Chilli Prawns

Our chilli prawns are a fiery and flavorful dish that's perfect for seafood lovers. Made with succulent prawns, stir-fried with a blend of aromatic spices, garlic, and fresh green chilies, this dish packs a punch of heat and flavor. Served with a side of steaming hot rice and a cooling cucumber raita, this dish is a perfect balance of spicy and cooling flavors. Our chilli prawns are not only delicious but also nutritious, as prawns are a good source of lean protein and omega-3 fatty acids. Experience the crunch and texture of this tasty dish, and let the spices tingle your taste buds.!
