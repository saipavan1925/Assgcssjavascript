King Fish Fry

Our king fish fry is a delicious and crispy appetizer that's perfect for seafood lovers. Made with fresh and juicy king fish fillets, marinated in a blend of spices and coated with a light batter before being fried to golden perfection. Served with a side of zesty mint chutney and a wedge of lemon, this dish is a perfect combination of savory and tangy flavors. Our king fish fry is not only delicious but also healthy, as fish is a great source of lean protein and omega-3 fatty acids. Experience the crunch and texture of this tasty dish and let the flavors explode in your mouth.
